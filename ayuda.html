Connecting Home Owners with Reliable Contractors

Client

Ayuda Ltd.

Role(s)

Product Designer

Date

October 2018 - December 2018

Brief

It’s a real difficulty, finding reliable, trustworth artisans and workers. This client, having worked in the construction business for several years realised he could build a network of plumbers, carpenters, etc. that can be known for the quality of their work and can be trusted to deliver on time. My job was to deisgn a set of applications for workers to receive job requests and consumers to launch request. The main challenge here was to foster trust as these connections are made.
