Hi, I’m Kobby 👋🏾

I’m an Accra-based product designer with more questions than answers. I believe our world will be a better place if things are intentionally designed to work for real people. As a designer I spend my time trying to understand the processes that take an idea from inception to execution. I have broad interests in podcasting, writing, politics, inequality, good governance, interior decoration and the internet of things, among other things.
