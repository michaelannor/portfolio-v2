Empowering Companies with Tools to Rethink Customer Service

Hubtel at the time was a company in pivot. From running a successful SMS messaging business the next direction was to go beyond messaging to a platform for businesses to wholistical engage their customers. This included adding on a Sales Platform and a CRM Platform to compliment messaging.

Client

Hubtel Ltd.

Role(s)

Product UX Designer

Date

December 2017 - March 2018

Brief

At the time, we had businesses who had been messaging (ads, alerts, receipts, etc.) their customers for years. What we learnt was customers had created various complex spreadsheets to manage their customers and uploading these to the messaging platform each time they wanted to send a message was cumbersome. Working closely with the product manager for this project we identified the needs of this new customer insights platform. The overarching goal was to provide business with insights to be more intentional about their interactions with their customers:
